Why would you want to use this? Synced Schedule makes planning activities and trips a lot easier and less time consuming.

Take the endless texting trying to figure out when all of your friends might have some time to get together. The website will block out the times that your friends are not available and color code each person so you can clearly and easily see who wouldn't be available at that time.
