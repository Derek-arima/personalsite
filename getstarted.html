Get started by creating an account and adding your account detail. You can then add your friends and create a calender for your group. The calender will show when your friends are available and when they are not. You can then create events and invite your friends to them.

The events will show up on your friends calender and they can accept or decline the event. If they accept the event will show up on their calender and they will be able to see the event details. If they decline the event will not show up on their calender and they will not be able to see the event details.
